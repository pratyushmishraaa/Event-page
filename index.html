Events and Shows

Arijit Singh is one of the most loved and famous singers from India. In 2005, he was among the top six contestants on the reality singing show Fame Gurukul, and later, he went on to work as an assistant to music director Pritam. Arijit Singh, known for his deep voice that resounds with love tunes, is breaking new ground in the music industry by conjuring unforgettable notes for youthful hearts. On April 25, 1987, Arijit was born in Murshidabad, Jiaganj, West Bengal, India.
Arijit's house was filled with lovers of music and talented singers. His mother and grandmother were excellent singers, and his mother was also a s skilled tabla player. His maternal uncle was also a tabla player. This musical environment at home influenced him so much so that Arijit decided that he would pursue a profession in music as well. Rajendra Prasad taught Arijit classical music, while Dhirendra Prasad Hazari taught him the art of tabla playing. Birendra Prasad Hazari composed pop music for Arijit.
